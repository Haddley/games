GAME NIGHT

multiplayer browser games — no installs, phones as controllers

🎉
BOGGLE PARTYnew
1–8 players race to swipe words on their phones while the TV keeps score. Earn prank tokens and hit rivals with ink splats, dizzy spins and freezes. Music, fireworks, QR-code joining.
📖
FAMILY TRIVIAnew
The whole family reads a short story on the TV, then answers a multiple-choice question in secret on their phones. Be right when everyone else is wrong for the Lone Wolf payout.
🤥
FIBBERS!new
Bluff trivia: everyone invents a fake answer, then votes for the truth among the lies. Score for spotting the truth — and for every player your lie fools.
🎨
DOODLE PARTYnew
Sketch a secret word on your phone while it appears live on the TV — everyone else races to guess it first. Letter hints, near-miss nudges, drawer rotation.
🐑
THE ODD SHEEPnew
Everyone secretly knows the word — except one faker. Drop one-word clues, argue, vote out the sheep… who might still guess their way to victory.
🐮
HERD MINDnew
Answer like the family would: the biggest herd scores, and the one lone answer wins the Pink Cow — which blocks you from winning until you pass it on.
⚡
CATEGORY CLASHnew
One letter, eight categories, one frantic timer. Unique answers score — duplicates CLASH and cancel. The captain gets a veto for dodgy answers.
🎯
BEST GUESSnew
Trivia for people who don't know trivia: everyone guesses a number, then bets on whichever guess looks closest. Grandma can win by betting on you.
✏️
BROKEN PENCILnew
The telephone game in drawings: write, draw, describe, redraw… then watch every chain fall apart on the TV, stroke by stroke. Vote the funniest beat.
🌙
MOONLIGHT VILLAGEnew
Werewolf with the TV as narrator: secret roles on phones, night actions, dawn reveals and dramatic day votes. Wolves lurk among the family.
🔨
GOING, GOING, GONE!new
A live auction of ridiculous lots with hidden values. Outbid the family before the gavel falls, then survive the valuation: treasure or haunted toaster?
🎫
FULL HOUSE BINGOnew
The TV is the caller — ball machine, nickname calls and flashboard. Daub your ticket on your phone and smash BINGO! for the line and the full house.
🔤
BOGGLE
Classic multiplayer Boggle — tap out words on a shared 4×4 board, duplicates cancel, longest unique words win the round.
🎲
LIAR'S DICE
Bid, bluff and call liar. Each player peeks at their own dice on their phone — last one with dice standing wins.
🔔
PIT!redone
The frantic commodity-trading game, rebuilt party-style: shout-trade card bundles on your phone while the TV runs the trading floor. QR joining, corner bell, Bull & Bear traps.
⭕
TIC TAC TOE
Head-to-head noughts and crosses over peer-to-peer — share a room code and play.
🪂
SKYDIVE
Solo arcade freefall — dodge, dive and stick the landing.
🌆
SYNTHWAVE
Neon retro arcade shooter with a full synth soundtrack.
